FAQ

Have a question? Check out our list of FAQs below, or contact us and we will be sure to answer your query as best we can!

Is face-to-face study better than online study?

This is a very common question. Choosing whether to study online or face-to-face is very much a personal choice and dependent on what suits you and your current other work, study and lifestyle. Therefore, the choice to complete your studies either face-to-face or online is really up to the individual, as both options are great ways to study. commitments.

How am I supported throughout my course?

Our Student Support team ensure you receive all the help you need to get you through your studies. Students studying online can simply send an email or pick up the phone to gain the support they need or request assistance. Throughout the online course, there are regular webinars which are like a live interactive tutorial. All of our Student Support staff are highly qualified and experienced fitness professionals.

What are the job opportunities and prospects within the fitness industry?

There is a growing number of Australians joining Fitness Centres across the country, with many requiring the services of a Gym Instructor or Personal Trainer to achieve their fitness goals. Employment opportunities are many and varied, including larger fitness centres, personal training studios, outdoor boot camp training, local government leisure centres, corporate fitness facilities, community health centres, hotels, resorts and more.

Is there a payment plan available to pay the course fees?

Yes, we have payment plans available for our courses. Course fees are both affordable and flexible through our interest-free payment options allowing you to make weekly or fortnightly payments to fit in with your budget. Please contact for more specific information.
